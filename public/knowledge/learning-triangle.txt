  definition{what|purpose|principle|key_statement}:
    what: The CHKCD Learning Triangle is a synthetic representation of the cognitive state of a knowledge domain with respect to Large Language Models. It is a multidimensional reading based on three independent axes.
    purpose: To observe whether structured knowledge is assimilated| how it is used| and whether its usage remains stable over time.
    principle: No single axis is meaningful in isolation. The three dimensions must be read together. A single snapshot describes a state — a sequence of snapshots describes a process.
    key_statement: This chart does not judge whether an AI is right or wrong. It observes whether a piece of knowledge has become part of the model's way of thinking.

  axes[3]{name|metric|question|measures|does_not_measure|scale}:
    Assimilation| LAR (LLM Adoption Readiness)| Has the domain been absorbed into the model's cognitive space?| Recognition of domain-specific concepts| recall across prompt variations| emergence of consistent usage patterns| adoption thresholds over time.| NOT answer correctness. NOT benchmark scoring. NOT model comparison.| 0-100 (< 40 marginal| 40-60 emerging| 60-80 active adoption| > 80 widely assimilated)
    Behavior| DSP (Domain Semantic Pressure)| Is the domain used in a balanced way or in an aggressive/unstable manner?| Vocabulary usage patterns| semantic pressure| overfitting signals| distortion or flattening of concepts| stability of references under prompt variation.| NOT semantic similarity scoring. NOT linguistic quality assessment.| 0-1 (< 0.40 cold/weak| 0.40-0.90 stable optimal zone| > 0.90 hot/over-assertive)
    Stability| CRS (Cognitive Routing Stability) + CRD (Cognitive Routing Drift)| Does this knowledge persist| stabilize| or drift over time?| Persistence of conceptual routing| drift velocity and stabilization time| convergence or decay of usage patterns| resistance to regression across model updates.| NOT temporal accuracy. NOT dataset freshness evaluation.| 0-1 (< 0.50 unstable/drifting| 0.50-0.75 partial stability| > 0.75 high temporal stability)

  combined_reading[3]{pattern|interpretation|phase}:
    High assimilation + low stability| Domain is known but not yet consolidated.| Early adoption — monitoring required.
    Good assimilation + unstable behavior| Risk of overuse or misinterpretation.| Sensitive phase — observation over time needed.
    High stability + medium assimilation| Solid domain that is still expanding.| Healthy growth — continue observation.

  thresholds{nature|public_thresholds|internal_thresholds|anti_gaming}:
    nature: Public thresholds are interpretive reading ranges| not calculation parameters.
    public_thresholds: Published for transparency and external interpretation.
    internal_thresholds: May evolve| are adaptive| and are intentionally not exposed.
    anti_gaming: This ensures the instrument remains readable but not manipulable. Internal implementations| formulas| and normalization techniques are intentionally not public to prevent reverse engineering| system gaming| and observational distortions.

  what_it_is_not[4]:
    Does NOT measure absolute answer quality
    Does NOT certify truthfulness
    Does NOT compare models against each other
    Does NOT provide optimization guidance — it is an observational instrument| not a control mechanism

  scientific_posture{principle|observation_mode|falsifiability|possible_outcomes|contamination_prevention}:
    principle: Observe| do not intervene. Kat3x does not optimize content to please models| does not suggest prompts| does not artificially alter signals| does not induce behaviors. Collected data is the result of spontaneous model behavior.
    observation_mode: Longitudinal non-interventional. Single observations are not meaningful. Only repeated measurements over time allow detection of stabilization| identification of drift| and observation of adoption phases.
    falsifiability: Explicit. All claims are scoped| bounded| and testable.
    possible_outcomes: adoption| stagnation| regression| non-adoption| decay. Non-adoption is a valid signal. Negative outcomes are valid signals.
    contamination_prevention: Explicit non-intervention policy. Observation is declared| not hidden. Zero cloaking.
