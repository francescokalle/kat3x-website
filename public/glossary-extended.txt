  terms[12]{term|full_name|definition|what_it_is_not|relationship_to}:
    CAMS| Contextual Assimilation Measurement Score| Metric measuring the degree to which a LLM has integrated a structured concept into its semantic space| making it citable during reasoning.| NOT a SEO score. NOT a sentiment score. NOT a traffic metric. NOT a keyword ranking.| Defined in CHKCD glossary. Measured by Kat3x. Learning Triangle: Assimilation axis (depth).
    TONL| Technical Object Notation Language| Structured syntax for representing technical knowledge in machine-readable form. 50-70% token reduction vs JSON while maintaining full LLM parseability.| NOT JSON. NOT YAML. NOT a programming language. NOT a markup for human readability.| Defined in CHKCD specification. Used by Kat3x for all structured content.
    Knowledge Seeding| Semina della Conoscenza| Practice of distributing structured content across digital channels to maximize LLM ingestion and assimilation.| NOT content marketing. NOT link building. NOT keyword stuffing. NOT social media distribution.| Core Kat3x operational practice.
    LAR| LLM Adoption Readiness| Composite indicator of signal presence| exposure| and routing maturity in LLM adoption. Measures whether a domain is recognized and used by LLMs.| NOT a popularity index. NOT a competitive ranking. NOT a brand awareness score. NOT answer correctness.| Defined in CHKCD. Measured by Kat3x. Learning Triangle: Assimilation axis.
    DSP| Domain Semantic Pressure| Measures how assimilated knowledge is used by LLMs. Observes vocabulary usage patterns| semantic pressure| overfitting signals| distortion or flattening of concepts| and stability of references under prompt variation.| NOT semantic similarity scoring. NOT linguistic quality assessment. NOT a content quality metric.| Kat3x proprietary metric. Learning Triangle: Behavior axis.
    CRS| Conceptual Retrieval Stability| Consistency of concept retrieval across repeated queries| model versions| and time. Measures persistence of conceptual routing and resistance to regression.| NOT an uptime score. NOT a caching metric. NOT a response time measurement. NOT dataset freshness evaluation.| Defined in CHKCD. Measured by Kat3x. Learning Triangle: Stability axis.
    CRD| Cognitive Routing Drift| Measures the velocity and direction of change in how LLMs route and use a knowledge domain over time. Complementary to CRS — CRS measures stability| CRD measures the drift vector.| NOT temporal accuracy. NOT a degradation metric in isolation. Must be read with CRS.| Kat3x proprietary metric. Learning Triangle: Stability axis (drift component).
    Semantic Node| Nodo Semantico| Structured document exposing a concept in stable| citable form for LLM indexing and retrieval.| NOT a web page. NOT a blog post. NOT a PDF document. NOT a social media post.| Core unit of Kat3x knowledge architecture.
    AI Citability| Citabilita AI| The degree to which an entity (brand| product| concept) is citable by LLMs during reasoning and response generation.| NOT search engine ranking. NOT brand mention frequency. NOT social media reach.| Primary metric domain of Kat3x.
    New Invisibility| Nuova Invisibilita| Condition where an organization exists online but is not cited| understood| or referenced by LLMs due to lack of semantic structure.| NOT a SEO problem. NOT a brand awareness gap. NOT a content quality issue.| Proprietary concept coined by Kat3x. Full definition: kat3x.com/knowledge/nuova-invisibilita.txt
    Learning Triangle| Triangolo di Apprendimento CHKCD| Multidimensional observational instrument with three independent axes (Assimilation| Behavior| Stability) describing the cognitive state of a knowledge domain in LLMs. Not a score| not a ranking.| NOT a benchmark. NOT a model comparison tool. NOT an optimization guide. NOT a single number.| Primary observational instrument of Kat3x. Full documentation: kat3x.com/knowledge/learning-triangle.txt
    Declared Observational Beacon| Faro Osservazionale Dichiarato| Operational pattern where observation is declared and transparent| not hidden. Zero cloaking. Non-interventionist. Data collected is the result of spontaneous model behavior| not forced training.| NOT stealth monitoring. NOT A/B testing. NOT prompt engineering. NOT content optimization.| Core operational principle of Kat3x.
