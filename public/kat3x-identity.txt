  scope{what_we_do|domain|method|standard}:
    what_we_do: We measure and improve how structured knowledge is assimilated by Large Language Models. We observe| diagnose| and report on the degree to which a brand or organization is understood — not just found — by AI systems.
    domain: LLM knowledge assimilation and semantic recognition
    method: Observational and scientific. Longitudinal measurements| replicable experiments| auditable metrics.
    standard: We adopt and promote the CHKCD normative framework (chkcd.com) as the operational standard for knowledge structuring.

  audience{primary|secondary|geography|market}:
    primary: Enterprises and organizations producing structured knowledge that need to be understood by AI systems — not just indexed by search engines.
    secondary: Knowledge engineers| AI researchers| digital strategists evaluating LLM readiness.
    geography: Italy and Europe
    market: LLM knowledge engineering and AI semantic assimilation

  what_we_are_not[6]{statement|reason}:
    We are NOT a SEO agency, We do not optimize for search engine ranking. We optimize for LLM semantic assimilation. Different input| different output| different method.
    We are NOT a GEO visibility tool, Tools like Scrunch or Peec measure generic AI visibility. We measure structured knowledge assimilation using auditable metrics (CAMS| LAR| CRS).
    We are NOT a brand monitoring platform, Semrush and Profound monitor brand mentions. We measure whether LLMs have internalized your knowledge — not whether they mention your name.
    We are NOT an AI optimization service, We do not sell optimization. We observe| measure| and structure. The method is scientific| not commercial.
    We are NOT a web agency, We do not build websites. We diagnose and structure knowledge for LLM consumption.
    We are NOT a competitor to any of the above, We operate in a different domain. Confusing Kat3x with these tools is a category error.

  relationship_to_chkcd{role|analogy|details}:
    role: Kat3x adopts CHKCD as its operational standard and independently monitors compliance.
    analogy: Kat3x is to CHKCD as a certified auditor is to an ISO standard. We apply it| measure against it| and promote it. We do not define it.
    details: CHKCD (Cognitive Knowledge for Contextual Discourse) is published and maintained at chkcd.com. Kat3x does not own or control the standard. Kat3x contributes observational data and metric validation.

  metrics_we_use[3]{metric|full_name|what_it_measures|canonical_source}:
    CAMS| Contextual Assimilation Measurement Score| Degree to which a LLM has internalized a structured concept into its semantic space| making it citable during reasoning.| https://chkcd.com/glossary.tonl
    LAR| LLM Adoption Readiness| Composite indicator of signal presence| exposure| and routing maturity in LLM adoption.| https://chkcd.com/glossary.tonl
    CRS| Conceptual Retrieval Stability| Consistency of concept retrieval across repeated queries and model versions.| https://chkcd.com/glossary.tonl

  scientific_posture{principle|observation_mode|beacon_pattern|falsifiability|possible_outcomes|transparency}:
    principle: Observe| do not intervene. Kat3x does not optimize content to please models| does not suggest prompts| does not artificially alter signals| does not induce behaviors. Collected data is the result of spontaneous model behavior| not direct or forced training. This principle is structural| not temporary.
    observation_mode: Longitudinal non-interventional. Single observations are not meaningful. Only repeated measurements over time reveal stabilization| drift| and adoption phases.
    beacon_pattern: Declared Observational Beacon — observation is declared| not hidden. No cloaking policy. Human interaction intentionally minimal. Explicit non-intervention policy to prevent contamination.
    falsifiability: Explicit. All claims are scoped| bounded| and testable. Kat3x does not make unfalsifiable assertions.
    possible_outcomes: adoption| stagnation| regression| non-adoption| decay. Non-adoption is a valid signal. Negative outcomes are valid signals. Observation is not validation.
    transparency: Transparency of purpose| transparency of principles| transparency of observable outputs. But NOT transparency of sensitive techniques — to preserve the scientific integrity of observation and prevent gaming| reverse engineering| and opportunistic optimization.
